Office Rulebook

1. Work Hours

  • Regular Timings: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Mon-Fri).
  • Break Time: 1:30 PM - 2:30 PM (Lunch).
  • Flexible Hours: Permitted with approval.

2. Attendance & Punctuality

  • Mark daily attendance in the team system.
  • Late arrivals (>9:30 AM) noted after 3 instances/month.

3. Work Mode

  • Arrangement: On-Site Work Only.
  • Daily Stand-up: 9:15 AM.
  • EOD Updates: Submit by 5:15 PM.

4. Professional Conduct

  • Respect hierarchy and maintain professionalism.
  • Address seniors, mentors, and leads with courtesy.
  • Use proper channels for concerns.

5. Communication

  • Use Google Meet & WhatsApp for work updates.
  • Communication must be clear and respectful.

11. Personal Development

  • Pursue one growth activity per quarter.

8. Leaves & Time Off

  • Inform 1 day prior for planned leaves.

  • Embrace positivity, team spirit, and mutual support.
